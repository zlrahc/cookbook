Crispy Whole Fried Chicken

Ingredients

1.2 kg Whole Chicken
4 tbsp Fish Sauce
2 tbsp Knorr Chicken Broth Base
1 tbsp Peppercorns
5 pcs Bay Leaves
1 bulb Whole Garlic
2 tbsp Knorr Liquid Seasoning
Salt
Oil

Instructions

STEP 1

In a pot, bring to a boil all the ingredients except the chicken.

STEP 2

Boil for 15 minutes to infuse the water and add the whole chicken.

STEP 3

Bring to a boil for 5 minutes then lower the heat and simmer for 45 minutes or until the internal temperature reaches 165°F or 75°C.

STEP 4

Remove the chicken from the stock and cool down completely (for best results, cool down in the stock overnight).

STEP 5

Bring the deep frying oil to a temperature of 190°C and deep fry the whole chicken until it is very crispy (about 20 minutes).

STEP 6

Drain the fat and serve.
