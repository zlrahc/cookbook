Tinola

Ingredients

1 tbsp Canola oil
1 small Onion, peeled and sliced thinly
3 cloves Garlic, peeled and minced
2 thumb-sized Fresh ginger
1 (3 to 4 pounds) Whole chicken, cut into serving pieces
2 tbsp Fish sauce
5 cups Water
1 small Green papaya, pared, seeded, and cut into 2-inch wedges
1 bunch Fresh spinach leaves, stems trimmed
As needed Salt and pepper

Instructions

STEP 1

In a pot over medium heat, heat oil. Add onions, ginger, and garlic. Cook until softened.

STEP 2

Add chicken and cook, stirring occasionally, for about 5 to 7 minutes or until chicken starts to change color and its juices run clear.

STEP 3

Add fish sauce and cook, stirring occasionally, for about 1 to 2 minutes.

STEP 4

Add water and bring it to a boil. Skim off any scum that floats on top. Lower the heat, cover, and simmer for about 30 to 35 minutes or until the chicken is fully cooked.

STEP 5

Add papaya and cook for about 3 to 5 minutes or until it reaches a tender yet crisp texture.

STEP 6

Season with salt and pepper to taste.

STEP 7

Add spinach to the pot, pushing it down into the broth. Cook until the spinach is just wilted. Serve the dish hot.
