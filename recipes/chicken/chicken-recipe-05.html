Afritada

Ingredients

¼ cup Canola oil
2 medium Potatoes, peeled and quartered
1 large Carrot, peeled and cut into cubes
½ small Red bell pepper, cored and sliced into ½-inch thick strips
½ small Green bell pepper, cored and sliced into ½-inch thick strips
4 pounds Chicken, cut into serving pieces
1 small Onion, peeled and sliced thinly
3 cloves Garlic, peeled and minced
6 large Roma tomatoes
1 tbsp Fish sauce
1 cup Water
½ cup Frozen sweet peas, thawed
As needed Salt and pepper

Instructions

STEP 1

In a pan over medium heat, heat oil.

STEP 2

Add potatoes and carrots. Cook, turning once or twice, until lightly browned. Remove from pan and drain on paper towels.

STEP 3

Remove excess oil from the pan, leaving about 2 tablespoons.

STEP 4

Add bell peppers and cook for about 30 to 45 seconds. Remove from pan and drain on paper towels.

STEP 5

Add onions and garlic. Cook until softened.

STEP 6

Add chicken. Cook, turning on sides once or twice, until lightly browned but not fully cooked through.

STEP 7

Add tomatoes. Cook, mashing regularly with the back of a spoon until softened and releasing juices.

STEP 8

Add fish sauce. Cook for about 1 to 2 minutes.

STEP 9

Add water and bring to a boil.

STEP 10

Lower the heat, cover, and cook for about 20 to 30 minutes or until the chicken is cooked through and the sauce is thickened to the desired consistency.

STEP 11

Add potatoes and carrots. Continue to cook until fork-tender.

STEP 12

Add bell peppers and sweet peas. Cook for 1 to 2 minutes or until green peas are heated through and bell peppers are tender yet crisp.

STEP 13

Season with salt and pepper to taste. Serve hot.
