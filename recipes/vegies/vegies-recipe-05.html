Ginisang Ampalaya with Shrimp

Ingredients

4 medium Ampalaya
1 tbsp Canola oil
1 Onion, peeled and thinly sliced
2 cloves Garlic, peeled and minced
2 large Roma tomatoes, chopped
1 tbsp Fish sauce
½ pound Medium shrimp, peeled and deveined
1 cup Water
2 Eggs, beaten
½ tsp Sugar
As needed Salt and pepper

Instructions

STEP 1

Cut ampalaya lengthwise and with a spoon, remove seeds and scrape off white pith. Slice thinly and place in a bowl of cold water until needed. Drain well when ready to use.

STEP 2

In a wide skillet over medium heat, heat oil. Add onions and garlic. Cook, stirring regularly, until softened.

STEP 3

Add tomatoes and cook, mashing with the back of the spoon, until softened and releasing juice. Add fish sauce and cook for about 1 minute.

STEP 4

Add shrimp and continue to cook, stirring occasionally, until shrimp starts to change color.

STEP 5

Add water and bring to a boil.

STEP 6

Add ampalaya and gently toss to combine. Cook for about 2 to 3 minutes or until tender yet crisp.

STEP 7

In a thin stream, add eggs and gently stir to distribute. Continue to cook for about 1 minute or until eggs have set.

STEP 8

Season with sugar, salt, and pepper to taste. Serve hot.
