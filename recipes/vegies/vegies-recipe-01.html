Vegetable Stir Fry

Ingredients

1 tbsp Oil
1 med Onion, sliced thin
1 cup Diagonally sliced carrots
2 cups Broccoli florets
2 cups Sugar snap peas
1 large Red bell pepper, cut into strips
1 tbsp Reduced sodium soy sauce
1 tsp Garlic Powder
1 tsp Ground Ginger
2 tsp Sesame Seed, toasted

Procedures

STEP 1

Heat oil in a wok or large deep skillet over medium-high heat. Add onion and carrots; stir-fry for 2 minutes.

STEP 2

Add the remaining vegetables and continue stir-frying for 5 to 7 minutes, or until the vegetables reach a tender-crisp texture.

STEP 3

Introduce soy sauce, garlic powder, and ginger to the vegetables. Stir-fry until the ingredients are well blended.

STEP 4

Sprinkle the stir-fry with sesame seeds. Optionally, serve it over cooked rice.
