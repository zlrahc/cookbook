Vegetable Soup

Ingridients

2 tbsp Extra-virgin olive oil
1 med Yellow onion, diced
1 tsp Sea salt
As needed Freshly ground black pepper
1 med Carrot, diced
1 small Sweet potato, diced
1 can (14.5 oz) Fire roasted diced tomatoes
4 cloves Garlic, chopped
2 tsp or 2 tbsp Dried oregano, or chopped fresh thyme or rosemary
¼ tsp Red pepper flakes
4 cups Vegetable broth
2 Bay leaves
1 cup Halved cherry tomatoes
1 cup Chopped green beans
1 Zucchini, diced
1 can (15 oz) Chickpeas, drained and rinsed
2 tbsp White wine vinegar
1½ cups Chopped kale

Procedures

STEP 1

Heat oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add onion, salt, and several grinds of pepper. Cook, stirring occasionally, for 8 minutes. Then, add the carrot and sweet potato, stir, and cook for an additional 2 minutes.

STEP 2

Add canned tomatoes, garlic, oregano, and red pepper flakes to the pot. Stir in the broth and bay leaves. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat to a simmer. Cover and cook for 20 minutes.

STEP 3

Stir in cherry tomatoes, green beans, zucchini, and chickpeas. Cover and cook for an additional 10 to 15 minutes, or until the green beans are tender. Then, stir in the vinegar and kale, cooking for an additional 5 minutes until the kale is wilted. Season the dish to taste and serve.
