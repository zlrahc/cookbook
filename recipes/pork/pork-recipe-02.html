Pork Adobo

Ingredients

2 lbs Pork belly or pork shoulder, cut into cubes
1 cup Soy sauce
1/2 cup Vinegar (white or cane)
1 cup Water
4 cloves Garlic, minced
1 tsp Whole peppercorns
3 Bay leaves
To taste Ground black pepper
2 tbsp Oil

Procedures

STEP 1

In a bowl, combine pork, soy sauce, minced garlic, peppercorns, and bay leaves. Marinate for at least 30 minutes to an hour.

STEP 2

Heat oil in a pan over medium heat. Add the marinated pork and cook until browned.

STEP 3

Pour the remaining marinade mixture into the pan. Add water and vinegar. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer until the pork is tender (about 30-45 minutes).

STEP 4

Season with ground black pepper to taste. Continue simmering until the sauce thickens.

STEP 5

Serve hot with steamed rice. Enjoy your flavorful Pork Adobo!
