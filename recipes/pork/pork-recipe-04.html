Pork Menudo

Ingredients

1 lb Pork (shoulder or butt)
1 Onion, chopped
4 cloves Garlic, minced
1 Fresh tomato, chopped
1 cup Tomato sauce
2 tbsp Soy sauce
1 tbsp Fish sauce (optional)
1 cup Carrots, cubed
1 cup Potatoes, cubed
1 cup Peas
1 each Red and green bell peppers, diced
1/4 cup Raisins
1 cup Garbanzo beans
2 tbsp Brown sugar
2 tbsp Fresh lime or lemon juice
to taste Salt and pepper
2 Bay leaves

Procedures

STEP 1

Marinate the pork with lemon/lime juice, soy sauce, bay leaves, salt, and pepper for at least 30 minutes to a couple of hours.

STEP 2

In a Dutch oven or stew pot over medium-high heat, saut√© onion until translucent. Add garlic and cook for 2 minutes. Add the marinated pork and cook for 10 minutes or until no longer pink.

STEP 3

Stir in fish sauce and 1 cup of water. Cook covered for 10 minutes. Add tomatoes, tomato sauce, and enough water to cover the pork. Cook covered for 30-40 minutes, stirring occasionally.

STEP 4

Add potatoes, carrots, and brown sugar. Cover and cook for 15-20 minutes. Add water if needed.

STEP 5

15 minutes before the stew is done, add bell peppers, peas, garbanzo beans, and raisins.

STEP 6

Adjust for soy sauce to taste. Cook until the pork is fork-tender.
