Lechon Kawali

Ingredients

2 lbs Pork belly, skin-on, cut into large chunks
4 cups Water
1 tbsp Peppercorns
4 cloves Garlic, crushed
2 bay leaves
Salt As Needed
Oil

Procedures

STEP 1

In a large pot, combine pork belly, water, peppercorns, garlic, bay leaves, and salt. Bring to a boil, then simmer until pork is tender (about 45 minutes to 1 hour).

STEP 2

Remove the pork from the pot and let it air dry for about 15-20 minutes. You can also refrigerate it uncovered for a few hours or overnight for better results.

STEP 3

Heat oil in a deep pan or pot for frying. Once the oil is hot enough, carefully add the pork pieces and fry until golden brown and crispy.

STEP 4

Remove the crispy pork from the oil and let it drain on a paper towel-lined plate.

STEP 5

Serve hot with a dipping sauce such as vinegar with garlic, soy sauce, or your preferred sauce. Enjoy your crunchy Lechon Kawali!
