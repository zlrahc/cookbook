Sinigang

Ingredients

1 lb Pork belly or pork ribs, cut into chunks
8 cups Water
1 packet (about 1.4 oz) Sinigang tamarind soup base mix
2 cups Vegetables (radish, eggplant, okra, spinach, or string beans)
To taste Salt
1 Onion, quartered
2 Tomatoes, quartered
3 cloves Garlic, crushed
2 Green chili peppers (optional)

Procedures

STEP 1

In a large pot, bring water to a boil. Add pork chunks and simmer until tender (about 45 minutes to 1 hour).

STEP 2

Add onions, tomatoes, garlic, sinigang mix, and green chili peppers (if using). Simmer for 10 minutes.

STEP 3

Add radish and simmer for 5 minutes, then add the rest of the vegetables and simmer until cooked but still slightly crisp.

STEP 4

Season with salt to taste. Serve hot and enjoy with steamed rice.
